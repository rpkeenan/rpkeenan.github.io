About AMISS

AMISS is the Arizona Molecular ISM Survey with the SMT. We are undertaking a survey of millimeter wavelength emission lines that trace the mass and conditions of molecular gas in the interstellar medium (ISM) of around 160 galaxies in the local universe.

AMISS has used the Arizona Radio Observatory's Submillimeter Telescope to obtain CO(2-1) spectra of 160 galaxies and CO(3-2) spectra of a subset of 20 of these. In addition, CO(1-0) spectra from the IRAM 30M telescope are available for the full sample from xCOLDGASS.

We are in the process of preparing the data for public release in Fall 2022. Watch this space for further updates on the status of the project.
